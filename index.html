About the Team

FTC Team 8404 Quixilver Robotics is a competitive high school robotics team based in San Jose, CA. We’re passionate about STEM, teamwork, and building innovative robots for every FTC season.

Outside of competition, we focus on mentoring younger students, hosting STEM workshops, and engaging with our community to promote robotics. Our team culture is rooted in collaboration, creativity, and a drive to improve — both technically and personally — through every challenge we face.
